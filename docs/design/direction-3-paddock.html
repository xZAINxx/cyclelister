Why Paddock Works

One Committed Livery

A single accent — racing yellow on near-black — carries every status, chart and CTA. No second brand color to dilute it. Red appears only as a warning (needs-review, low confidence), so it always means "look here."

Plate Chips & Condensed Chrome

Race-number plates count the review queue and tag artboards. Barlow Condensed drives nav, labels and status chips — personality lives in the chrome, never in the data.

Sober Data, Legible Parts

Part numbers, prices and confidence sit in tabular mono for scannability. Photos get an implied duotone treatment so a cluttered shop bench reads as one clean system.

Diagonal Stripe, Used Once

The stripe motif appears in the header only — a nod to paddock signage without turning the tool into nostalgia cosplay. It stays professional software first.
